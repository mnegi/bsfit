BSF Institute of Technology

BSF INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

BSF Institute of Technology was established in the year 1992, in the premises of this Centre under the Administrative control of STC. This prestigious Institution of BSF was inagurated by the Honorable Home Minister Shri. S.B. Chavan on 14th Aug ‘ 92. The Institute having three disciplines of Engineering and one Non-Engineering. Courses such as:

Electronics & Communication Engineering (60 intake)
Electrical & Electronics Engineering (60 intake)
Computer Science & Engineering (60 intake)
Commercial Practice (30 intake)
Affiliated to the Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Karnataka and also approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

  • Mission

To be a Diploma Institute par excellence having infrastructure and intellectual capital of global standards while maintaining a nationalistic perspective.

To constantly strive towards imparting quality technical education to BSF Wards in keeping with the present and emerging demands and Inculcating social values in them so that they develop into competent professionals and conscientious citizens.
