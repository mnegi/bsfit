    ELIGIBLITY CONDITIONS

  • DIPLOMA IN ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGG. COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGG. AND ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGG.
    • Candidates should be wards of serving/deceased /retired BSF personnel/BSF Private Fund Employees, personnel of IB/State Police personnel of J & K, Punjab, Karnataka, Assam, Meghalaya, Manipur, Tripura, Mizoram and Nagaland states.   Dependent Blood relatives of serving BSF personnel are also eligible.  However, their admission will be at the discretion of the Competent Authority.
    • Candidates should have passed Class X or 10+2 pattern or equivalent with a minimum aggregate of 55% in Science and Maths and overall minimum aggregate of 45% in one sitting in respect of admission in ECE and CSE and with a minimum aggregate of 50% in Science and Maths and overall minimum aggregate of 45% for admission to EEE course.  Candidates belonging to SC and ST should get minimum aggregate of 40% in science and maths and overall minimum aggregate of 40%.
    • Candidates should be between the age of 15 to 21 years as on 1st Oct 2015. Upper age limit in respect of SC and ST candidates is relaxable by 5 years.
  • DIPLOMA IN MODERN OFFICE PROCEDURE (MOP)/ COMMERCIAL PRACTICE (CP)
    • Candidates should be wards of serving / deceased / retired BSF personnel/BSF Private Fund Employees, civilian staff, civilians and dependent blood relatives are also eligible.  However, their admission will be at the discretion of the Competent Authority.
    • Candidates should have passed Class-X or 10+2 pattern or equivalent with a minimum aggregate of 40% in English and overall minimum aggregate of 45% in one sitting.  Candidates belonging to SC and ST should get minimum aggregate of 40% in English and an overall minimum aggregate of 40%.
    • Candidates should be between the age of 15 to 21 years as on 1st oct 2015.  Upper age limit in respect of SC and ST candidates is relaxable by 5 years.

    GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

    • Admission to the course will be on the merit according to the marks obtained in class Xth or 10+2 pattern or equivalent examination. If no eligible candidates are available for the reserved seats, these seats will be treated as unreserved.
    • Candidates selected for admission shall have to produce following testimonials in original at the time of admission otherwise their seat will be filled up in next in the merit:-
      • Mark sheets of class X or XII as the case may be, along with certificate, provisional certificates of having passed the class issued by the concerned school.
      • They should bring their transfer certificates from their respective school/college last studied. (No provisional certificates, Migration certificate is accepted by DTE. Karnataka)
        • Transfer Certificate (TC) is must without which the admission will be cancelled.
        • The candidate who has completed 10+2 pattern has to produce 10th and 12th mark sheets.
      • Certificates of health issued by Medical Officer or registered Medical Practitioner. Candidates should not suffer from any chronic disease.
      • Character and Antecedent certificate issued by the school/college last attended.
      • SC/ST certificate issued by the Appropriate Authorities in the case SC/ST candidates.
    • Hostel facility is available for students as per availability. 2nd year and 3rd year students will be provided hostel facility only if their discipline state conforms to the standard prescribed by the Institute. They will not get hostel accommodation automatically in the II nd and III rd year due to shortage of accommodation. PT and evening sports is compulsory for all hostel students including II and III year students. They have to give an undertaking in this regard. Messing / hostel charges of the students of diploma courses will be borne by the parents, irrespective of their rank except for the wards of widows which will be met out of Central Education Fund. The hostel facility will be withdrawn if the ward commits any serious indiscipline act or fails in more than two subjects. Final discretion in this regard lies with Principal BSF IT.
